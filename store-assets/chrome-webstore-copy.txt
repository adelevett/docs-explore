Detailed Description
AnswerDock recreates a lightweight research sidebar for Google Docs.

What it does
- Instant answers from DuckDuckGo for quick research while you write
- One-click source visit, copy answer, and copy citation actions
- Search fallback when no instant answer exists
- Web search in new tab for users who prefer full web results
- Context menu capture from any page: copies selected text or links to your clipboard, ready to paste into your open Google Doc

Who it is for
- Students and researchers collecting quick references
- Writers who need lightweight citation support without leaving Docs
- Teams that miss the old AnswerDock workflow

How it works
1) Open the sidebar in Google Docs
2) Search a topic
3) Use instant answers or open your preferred search engine
4) Paste copied answer/citation into the doc

Privacy highlights
- No account required
- No custom backend
- Settings are stored locally in your browser
- Search terms are sent to DuckDuckGo only for instant-answer lookups

How users can send feedback to Google Docs
Send feedback directly inside Google Docs:
1) Open any document
2) Click Help
3) Click Help Docs improve
4) Submit your message

Suggested message
The Explore feature was extremely useful for research and citations. Current alternatives do not replace its workflow. Please consider bringing it back.
